# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Havit is used by individuals and families who self-host a private inventory system for physical assets, consumables, EDC items, locations, credentials, and virtual purchases. Mobile users are usually checking, finding, scanning, or quickly adding something while away from a desk.

## Product Purpose

Havit keeps a full household asset ledger in one place. Success means users can record an item quickly, find where it belongs, understand its lifecycle state, and manage reminders or credentials without relying on memory.

## Brand Personality

Calm, practical, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a focused personal operations tool, not a marketing site.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS dashboards, oversized hero layouts, novelty controls, and dense desktop tables squeezed into a phone viewport.

## Design Principles

- Mobile is a first-class PWA workflow for quick lookup, scan, and add actions.
- Preserve information hierarchy: title, tabs or filters, metrics, then the working list.
- Keep touch actions obvious and reachable, especially primary add and scan flows.
- Prefer compact product UI patterns over decorative cards when users are completing tasks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, keyboard focus visibility, touch targets around 44px where practical, and no interaction that depends on hover.
